Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Personal Budget App collects and processes the following types of information:

  • Financial Data: Bank account information, transaction details, and account balances through our integration with Plaid
  • Account Information: Email address and basic profile information
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our application

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing personal financial management services
  • Displaying your account balances and transaction history
  • Generating spending insights and budget reports
  • Ensuring the security and integrity of our services

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your financial data is:

  • Never shared with advertisers or marketing companies
  • Only accessed through secure, encrypted connections
  • Used exclusively for your personal financial management

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Secure Connections: We use bank-level security protocols
  • Plaid Integration: Financial data is accessed through Plaid's secure API
  • No Credential Storage: We never store your banking login credentials

5. Third-Party Services

We use Plaid Technologies, Inc. to connect to your financial institutions. Plaid's privacy policy governs how they handle your financial data. You can review Plaid's privacy policy at: https://plaid.com/legal/privacy

6. Data Retention

We retain your financial data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Disconnect financial accounts at any time

8. Personal Use

Personal Budget App is designed for individual, personal use. This application is not intended for commercial use or sharing with other users.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last modified" date.
